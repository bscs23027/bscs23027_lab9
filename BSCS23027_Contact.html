WHY OLAY

We believe that everyone has the power to Face Anything. We believe in a diverse and inclusive standard of beauty in which every person has the freedom to set their own definition of ‘beauty’ based on personal preferences, not societal expectations or bias. We believe in superior science. With over 65 years of a science-first approach to skin care we’re dedicated to giving you quality products, safe ingredients and real results.

THE OLAY SKIN PROMISE

Women are constantly facing conflicting expectations about their outward appearance. OLAY is making a commitment to zero skin retouching in all advertising by 2021, becoming the first mass skin care brand in the US to make this stance. Introducing the OLAY skin promise. This logomark will appear on all advertising to signify that the skin of the women featured has not been retouched.
